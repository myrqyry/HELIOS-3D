## 2. Empirical Support for Room-Temperature Hopfion Substrates

<ExpandableChunk 
  title="EuS/Bi₂Se₃/EuS Trilayers (Katmis et al., 2025)" 
  summary="First observation of room-temperature, zero-field magnetic hopfions."
>
Katmis et al., *Interface-Induced Stability of Nontrivial Topological Spin Textures* (Advanced Materials 2025), report the first observation of robust magnetic hopfions and skyrmions at **room temperature and zero magnetic field**. The stability is driven by proximity-enhanced magnetism at the **Topological Insulator (TI)** / **Ferromagnetic Insulator (FMI)** interface. `[DEMONSTRATED]`
</ExpandableChunk>

<ExpandableChunk 
  title="Topological Protection & Energy Barriers" 
  summary="Hopf index stability exceeding 50 kBT."
>
Analysis indicates that the topological protection of the Hopf index ($Q_H=1$) in optimized heterostructures provides an annihilation barrier exceeding **$50 k_B T$** at room temperature, meeting non-volatility requirements. `[DEMONSTRATED]`
</ExpandableChunk>

## 4. Advanced Manufacturing Feasibility

<ExpandableChunk 
  title="Volumetric 3D Printing (DISH)" 
  summary="Sub-second (0.6s) macro-scaffold creation at 19 µm resolution."
>
Wang et al., *Sub-second volumetric 3D printing by synthesis of holographic light fields* (Nature 2026), demonstrate Digital Incoherent Synthesis of Holographic light fields (DISH), achieving 19 µm printing resolution across a 1 cm depth in only 0.6 seconds. `[DEMONSTRATED]`

**In Situ Integration:** DISH's single-side illumination enables printing directly onto **fixed surfaces** without sample rotation, supporting the "Planar-First" hybrid pipeline.
</ExpandableChunk>

<ExpandableChunk 
  title="Flexible-Rigid Transition (Y-zipper)" 
  summary="Rapid and reversible assembly of 3D scaffolds."
>
Li et al. (2026) demonstrate a 3D-printed "Y-zipper" structure that enables rapid transition between flexible strips and rigid rods. `[DEMONSTRATED]`

**Significance:** Offers a potential path for **reconfigurable or deployable 3D scaffolds**, allowing complex architectures to be "zipped" into rigid operational geometry. `[PROPOSED]`
</ExpandableChunk>

<ExpandableChunk 
  title="Deterministic Nucleation (PRL 2026)" 
  summary="Quantized phase slips for on-demand soliton creation."
>
Shi et al. (PRL 2026) demonstrate the deterministic nucleation of 3D fractional solitons using a "twist reservoir" mechanism. Chen et al. (Nature Physics 2026) report **isolated magnetic hopfions** in FeGe triggered by femtosecond laser pulses. `[DEMONSTRATED]`
</ExpandableChunk>

## 5. Readout Infrastructure Benchmarks

<ExpandableChunk 
  title="Topological Orbital Hall Effect (TOHE)" 
  summary="The definitive electronic hallmark for 3D hopfions."
>
Göbel & Lounis (2025/2026) identify TOHE as the **electronic hallmark** for 3D magnetic hopfions. When a current passes through a hopfion, its 3D spin texture generates an emergent 3D electromagnetic field, creating a transverse orbital current. `[INFERRED for experiments]`
</ExpandableChunk>

<ExpandableChunk 
  title="Microwave Signal Fidelity (PRL 2026)" 
  summary="Preserving sub-GHz signatures against thermal noise."
>
Yam et al. (PRL 2026) demonstrate microwave quantum state preservation at temperatures up to 4 K with **59.9% fidelity**. 

**Significance:** Establishes a benchmark for the HELIOS-3D **microwave signal chain** and sub-GHz "breathing mode" readout. `[INFERRED]`
</ExpandableChunk>

## 6. Emerging Applications & Interfacial Physics

<ExpandableChunk 
  title="Chiral-Induced Spin Selectivity (CISS)" 
  summary="Isotopic fractionation via chiral magnetic interfaces."
>
Vardi et al. (Chem 2026) demonstrate that magnetic surfaces can filter isotopes ($^{13}C$ vs $^{12}C$) in chiral molecules. 

**Significance:** Since hopfions are inherently chiral, this provides a foundation for HELIOS-3D as a **programmable CISS interface** for bio-chemical sensing. `[PROPOSED]`
</ExpandableChunk>

<ExpandableChunk 
  title="High-Speed Switching Performance Floor" 
  summary="40-ps switching at 1.7 pJ/µm² energy density."
>
Tsai et al. (Science 2026) demonstrate deterministic octupole switching in **$Mn_3Sn$** using **40-ps pulses** with device endurance exceeding **$10^{11}$ cycles**. `[DEMONSTRATED]`

**Optical-to-Magnetic Interface:** Confirms switching via **60-ps photocurrent pulses** generated directly from telecom-band lasers.
</ExpandableChunk>

## 7. Planar Spintronic Interface and Control Candidates

<ExpandableChunk 
  title="Environmental Dielectric Tuning (Nature Physics 2026)" 
  summary="In-situ moiré control via high-dielectric substrates."
>
Gao et al. (2026) demonstrate that high-dielectric **SrTiO3** substrates can tune or suppress correlated moiré states via dielectric screening. 

**HELIOS Application:** Enables dynamic modulation of "trapping potential" depth for switchable mobility/stability. `[INFERRED]`
</ExpandableChunk>

<ExpandableChunk 
  title="Twistless Moiré Engineering (Cornell 2026)" 
  summary="Deterministic printing of moiré potentials."
>
Cornell researchers demonstrate creating moiré superlattices without manual twisting by using lithographically patterned **metal stressor films**.

**Significance:** Replaces manual stacking with standard semiconductor manufacturing, enabling deterministic printing of moiré trapping potentials. `[PROPOSED for 3D]`
</ExpandableChunk>
