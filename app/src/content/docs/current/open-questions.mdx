# 🔍 HELIOS-3D: Open Research Questions & Technical Blockers

This document tracks the critical uncertainties and technical challenges that must be addressed to move the HELIOS-3D architecture from a theoretical hypothesis to a verifiable prototype.

---

## 🏗️ 1. Materials & Fabrication Integration

<div class="grid md:grid-cols-2 gap-4 my-6">
  <VisualCueCard title="Conformal vdW Deposition" status="speculative">
    **Blocker:** Can high-quality vdW ferromagnets like $Fe_3GaTe_2$ maintain magnetic anisotropy when applied via ALD over complex 3D DISH/TPP scaffolds?
  </VisualCueCard>

  <VisualCueCard title="Thermal Expansion Mismatch" status="speculative">
    **Blocker:** Differential expansion between polymer scaffolds and magnetic coatings may lead to delamination or property drift during thermal cycling.
  </VisualCueCard>

  <VisualCueCard title="Y-zipper Alignment" status="proposed">
    **Blocker:** Can the 3D-printed zipping process achieve the sub-micron alignment precision required for continuous spintronic interconnects?
  </VisualCueCard>

  <VisualCueCard title="Moiré Reproducibility" status="proposed">
    **Update (June 2026):** Cornell's **strain-induced moiré** method mitigates manual twisting disorder. 
    **Blocker:** Precise calibration for magnetic vdW materials remains unverified; proposed mitigation via AI-surrogate modeling of large-scale atomic stacks.
  </VisualCueCard>

  <VisualCueCard title="BN Twist-Angle Gating" status="proposed">
    **Blocker:** Relative twist angles between 15° and 45° in dual-encapsulated hBN layers cause room-temperature ferroelectric-like gate screening and hysteresis.
    **Mitigation:** Enforce angular alignment outside the 15°–45° window to preserve SOT and electrostatic control.
  </VisualCueCard>
</div>

## 🌀 3. Topological Stability

<div class="grid md:grid-cols-2 gap-4 my-6">
  <VisualCueCard title="Hopfion Nucleation Barrier" status="demonstrated">
    **Update:** $EuS/Bi_2Se_3/EuS$ trilayers show zero-field stability at 300 K. 
    **Blocker:** Precize barriers for **sub-100nm confined 3D geometries** remain unverified.
  </VisualCueCard>

  <VisualCueCard title="Data Center Stability" status="speculative">
    **Blocker:** Is topological protection sufficient at $T > 350$ K? 
    **Metric:** Requires binding energy $E_B > 50 k_B T$ at max operating temperature.
  </VisualCueCard>

  <VisualCueCard title="Skyrmion-to-Hopfion Path" status="proposed">
    **Blocker:** Is the transformation from 2D strings to 3D knots topologically allowed without destroying the spin texture?
  </VisualCueCard>

  <VisualCueCard title="Super-Moiré Textures" status="proposed">
    **Blocker:** How does protection translate in non-planar geometries? Can stressor films be applied conformally to a DISH scaffold?
  </VisualCueCard>
</div>

## 🎯 4. Minimum Viable Demonstrator (MVD)

To validate the HELIOS-3D hypothesis, a series of incremental demonstrators are required:

1.  **Phase I (Materials):** Proof-of-concept deposition of $Fe_3GaTe_2$ on a TPP-refined surface with MFM verification.
2.  **Phase II (MCA Logic):** Single-track domain wall displacement driven by SOT with microwave readout.
3.  **Phase III (BRC Stochasticity):** Observation of skyrmion Brownian motion in a confined polymer micro-harbor.
4.  **Phase IV (Integration):** 3D routing between a single MCA gate and a single BRC reservoir.
